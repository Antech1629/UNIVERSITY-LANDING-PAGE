ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

CERTIFICATE LEVEL REQUIREMENTS

  • You should have a Minimum KCSE Grade of D-(minus) From a well known secondary school in the country
    • OR
  • Have a certificate in Artisan for a Respective Course

DIPLOMA LEVEL QUALIFICATION

  • Must have a mean grade of C(plain) and above for a respective course
    • OR
  • Have a certificate from a recognised institution in the country for an Artisan Achievement

DEGREE LEVEL REQUIREMENTS

  • Have A KCSE mean grade of C+(PLUS) and above
    • OR
  • A diploma in recognised institution and fully working real-time projects

MASTERS QUALIFICATION

  • Must have a Degree from a recognised University in the country and a strong Portofolio with real world projects.
